📚 Chapter Summary: At Caesarea Philippi, Jesus asked His disciples who people said He was, and Simon Peter proclaimed, "You are the Messiah, the Son of the living God." Jesus said this truth was revealed to Peter by His heavenly Father, not by flesh and blood. Revelation is God's personal loving communication of who He is and His plan to save us — and the Church teaches that Jesus is the perfect revelation of God's love. Jesus is both true God and true Man; because He is truly human, He becomes the Sacrament of God, meaning through Him God's loving presence is revealed to humankind.
Title of JesusWhat It Means
✝️ Messiah / ChristThe Anointed One promised and sent by God
👑 Son of the living GodJesus is truly divine, the Son of God Himself
📖 Revealer of God our FatherShows us who God is
🌟 Image and Word of GodJesus perfectly reflects God to us
🎯 Final Goal of God's RevelationOur ultimate destiny and hope
📌 REMEMBER Jesus is both true God and true Man — this is why He is called the Sacrament of God: through His humanity, we can see and experience God's love up close.
🧠 Memory Trick Jesus is the FACE we see when we ask, "What is God like?" Every title — Messiah, Son of God, Revealer, Image of God — answers the same question: Jesus shows us the Father.
📚 Chapter Summary: During times of persecution, early Christians used secret symbols to represent their faith in Jesus — symbols still found today in churches, altars, and vestments. The Apostles' Creed is a summary of the Church's core beliefs: that Jesus was conceived by the Holy Spirit, born of the Virgin Mary, suffered under Pontius Pilate, died, was buried, and rose on the third day.
SymbolMeaning
ΑΩ Alpha and OmegaThe beginning and the end
☧ Chi-RhoFirst two Greek letters of "Christ"
IHSFirst three Greek letters of "Jesus"
🐟 Ichthus (ΙΧΘΥΣ)A fish — a secret sign used by persecuted Christians
Belief in the Apostles' CreedWhat It Means
Conceived by the Holy SpiritJesus' conception was a miracle, not by ordinary means
Born of the Virgin MaryMary is honored as the Mother of Jesus
Suffered, died, was buriedJesus truly suffered and died for our sins
Rose on the third dayJesus conquered death, giving us hope of eternal life
📌 REMEMBER These symbols weren't just decoration — they were a quiet, coded way for Christians to recognize each other and hold on to their faith during dangerous times.
🧠 Memory Trick Christians hid their faith in symbols the way a fish hides in water — quiet, but always there. And remember: Alpha-Omega = All Over (beginning to end)!
📚 Chapter Summary: National Heroes Day is celebrated every last Monday of August, honoring both heroes of the past and modern-day Filipinos who serve others. Three inspiring examples are Efren Peñaflorida (2009 CNN Hero of the Year, known for the kariton klasrum), Cris "Kesz" Valdez (received the International Children's Peace Prize for helping street children), and Cherrie De Erit Atilano (recognized for her work in agribusiness through AGREA).
Modern-Day HeroAdvocacy / Recognition
🛒 Efren PeñafloridaKariton klasrum; founded DTC (1997); 2009 CNN Hero of the Year
👦 Cris "Kesz" ValdezInternational Children's Peace Prize (2012); founded C3 — Gifts of Hope
🌾 Cherrie De Erit AtilanoOutstanding Young Filipino for Agribusiness (2018); founded AGREA
📌 REMEMBER A hero isn't only someone from history books — anyone who serves others selflessly today, like these three Filipinos, is a modern-day hero too.
🧠 Memory Trick "Efren teaches, Kesz gives, Cherrie grows" — one simple line to remember each hero's cause: education, giving, and farming.
📚 Chapter Summary: When Mary was engaged to Joseph and found to be with child through the Holy Spirit, Joseph planned to quietly divorce her — until the angel of the Lord appeared to him in a dream, telling him to name the Child Jesus, "because He will save His people from their sins." The name Jesus means Savior, Redeemer, or Deliverer. Jesus was proclaimed Redeemer even before His birth — His death on the Cross later became the ultimate symbol of His saving love.
TermMeaning
✝️ RedeemerOne who frees us from sin, like a hero
🙏 SaviorOne who saves us
🕊️ DelivererOne who rescues or frees us
📌 REMEMBER Jesus was proclaimed Redeemer even before His birth — the angel's message to Joseph already revealed His saving mission.
🧠 Memory Trick JESUS = Savior, Redeemer, Deliverer — three words, one mission: to save us from sin.
